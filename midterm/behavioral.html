Behavioral Questions

How to prepare

Question Categories 

Building relationships

  • Tell me about a time when you were not able to build a successful relationship with a difficult person.
  • What have you done in past situations to contribute toward a teamwork environment?
  • Tell us about a time when you built rapport quickly with someone under difficult conditions.

Communication

  • Describe a situation where you felt you had not communicated well. How did you correct the situation?
  • Tell me about a specific instance when you and your current/previous supervisor disagreed but you still found a way to get your point across.
  • What kinds of communication situations cause you difficulty? Give an example and explain why.

Initiative

  • Give some instances in which you anticipated problems and were able to influence a new direction. What was the end result?
  • Can you provide a specific example of when you found it necessary to devote extra time and energy to the job?
  • What sorts of projects did you generate that required you to go beyond your job description? How did you manage the extra work?

Leadership

  • Please provide an example of a situation where you have experienced difficulty getting others to accept your ideas. What was your approach? Did it work?
  • Give an example of a time in which you felt you were able to build motivation in your co-workers and/or subordinates at work.
  • Describe a situation in which you were able to successfully influence someone to see things your way. What was it that made your efforts successful?
